## What Zero Covers

Zero is an AIGC tutorial site for professionals. It focuses on practical onboarding: how beginners understand AI concepts, how teams actually use AI tools, and how outputs become reliable deliverables.

## Who This Is For

- Beginners who want a structured way to learn AIGC instead of chasing fragmented tips
- Professionals applying AI to writing, research, documentation, and coding tasks
- Learners building reusable prompt patterns, review habits, and workflow guardrails

## What You Can Learn Here

- [Concepts](/en/ai/concepts/): understand AIGC, prompts, and model limitations
- [Tools](/en/ai/tools/): practical tool walkthroughs for writing, documentation, and coding
- [Methodology](/en/ai/methodology/): reusable prompt structures, review steps, and acceptance criteria
- [Cases](/en/ai/cases/) and [Field Notes](/en/ai/logs/): real task breakdowns, mistakes, and fixes
- [Open Source](/en/ai/opensource/) and [Resources](/en/ai/resources/): higher-signal projects and learning materials

## Recommended Reading Order

1. Start with [Concepts](/en/ai/concepts/).
2. Move to [Tools](/en/ai/tools/) for scenario-based usage.
3. Use [Methodology](/en/ai/methodology/) to define prompts and review standards.
4. Then follow [Cases](/en/ai/cases/) and [News](/en/news/) to keep your workflow current.
